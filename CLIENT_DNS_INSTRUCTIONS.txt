========================================
DNS SETUP INSTRUCTIONS - HOSTINGER
========================================
Domain: massaba.app



STEP 1: LOGIN TO HOSTINGER

Go to: https://www.hostinger.com
Login with your credentials


STEP 2: FIND DNS ZONE EDITOR

1. Click "Domains" in left sidebar
2. Click "Manage" next to massaba.app
3. Click "DNS / Nameservers" tab
4. Click "DNS Zone Editor"


STEP 3: ADD A RECORD

Type: A
Name: @
Points to: 76.76.21.21
TTL: 14400

Click "Add Record"


STEP 4: ADD CNAME RECORD

Type: CNAME
Name: www
Points to: cname.vercel-dns.com
TTL: 14400

Click "Add Record"


STEP 5: ADD TXT RECORD (VERIFICATION)

Type: TXT
Name: @
Points to: vc-domain-verify=YOUR_CODE_HERE
TTL: 14400

Click "Add Record"

VERIFICATION CODE: [Paste code from Vercel here]


STEP 6: WAIT

Wait 5-30 minutes for DNS to update
The website will automatically go live at:
https://massaba.app
